Bittle Battle Baseball

Bittle Battle Baseball is a baseball simulator based upon real historical season statistics for every player. You can choose any two teams from any season going back to 1876 to play against one another in a head-to-head simulation where you will get to manage the game, see each play take place, and find out the ultimate outcome of the game. Outcomes are based on algorithms of statistical probabilities. LET HISTORY COME ALIVE!!!
