Bittle Battle Baseball

Bittle Battle Baseball is a baseball simulator based upon real historical stats.... You can choose any two teams to play against one another in a head-to-head simulation where you will get to see each play take place and find out the ultimate outcome of the game. LET HISTORY COME ALIVE!!!
